About This Project

Built for Players,
Powered by Data

PingSense aggregates real user reviews from the global table tennis community to help players make smarter equipment decisions. Whether you're a beginner looking for your first rubber or an advanced competitor optimizing your setup, our database gives you the numbers you need.

Every rubber in our database is rated across 12 performance dimensions — from raw Speed and Spin to the subtler qualities like Throw Angle, Gears, and long-term Durability.
